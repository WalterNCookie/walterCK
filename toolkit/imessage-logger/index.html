About

What it is and why it exists

iMessage Logger is a Shortcuts-powered archiving tool that captures your conversations as clean, readable logs — stored entirely on your device with no cloud required.

Built for anyone who wants a personal record of their messages without relying on iCloud backups or third-party apps. Run it once or automate it daily — either way, your conversations stay yours.

Features

What it does for you

Fully Private

Everything stays on-device. No internet, no third-party storage, no accounts required.

Clean Archive Format

Logs are saved as readable files you can open in Notes, Files, or any text viewer.

Automatable

Plug it into a Personal Automation to log on a schedule — daily, weekly, whatever works.

Choose Your Conversations

Select any contact or group chat to log. Process one thread or run through a list.
