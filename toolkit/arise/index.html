About

What it is and why it exists

Arise is an entirely automated shortcut built around one core assumption. On the hardest days, you want the easiest comfort. A school morning has no upside, and to get out of bed takes away half your energy before the day has even begun.

Built on top of pre-designed systems, Arise is a tap and play, no-experience required shortcut, giving you the most capibilties to set your mornings before they happen. It gives a sense of order, and comforts in the peace of knowledge of your day, while tracking your sleep and dreams for improvement and trend identification.

Features

What it does for you

Persistent

4 modes. Every one does not let the alarm switch off until you are up and focused, forcing you to start your day.

Dynamic Alarm Times

Your alarm is 6am one day, then 5am the next? You're sick and don't need a work alarm tomorrow? The shortcut manages it all, relax.

Tracks Sleep

Run the shortcut before bed with a click, and every day will have data, completely saved, on your sleep duration and patterns. No limits, complete privacy.

Tracks Dreams

There's an option for tracking dreams, keeping you mindful, and for those wanting a goal to chase even in their sleep. Cleanly, and easily this is integrated into the morning overview/alarm.

Calendar

Syncs with your calendar everyday to present you the events in your morning overview/alarm, immediently calming you, knowing the date, time and the day's outlook.

Builds the Habit

The consistency compounds. Run it every morning and the routine becomes second nature within a week.
