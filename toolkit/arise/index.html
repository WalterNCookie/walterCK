About

What it is and why it exists

Arise is an entirely automated shortcut built around one core assumption. On the hardest days, you want the easiest comfort. A school morning has no upside, and getting out of bed takes half your energy before the day has even begun.

Built on pre-designed systems, Arise is a tap-and-play, no-experience-required shortcut that gives you the most capability to set your mornings before they happen. It provides a sense of order and comfort through the knowledge of your day ahead, while tracking your sleep and dreams for improvement and trend identification.

Features

What it does for you

Persistent

4 modes. Every one refuses to let the alarm switch off until you are up and focused, forcing you to start your day.

Dynamic Alarm Times

6am one day, 5am the next? Sick and don't need a work alarm? The shortcut manages it all — relax.

Tracks Sleep

Run it before bed with one tap. Every day builds data on your sleep duration and patterns — private, no limits.

Tracks Dreams

Log dreams each morning, cleanly integrated into the overview. Stay mindful and spot patterns over time.

Calendar

Syncs with your calendar daily, presenting your events in the morning alarm — you wake knowing exactly what lies ahead.

Builds the Habit

The consistency compounds. Run it every morning and the routine becomes second nature within a week.
